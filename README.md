# üß© 2048: Flow Game

**2048: Flow Game** is an innovative version of the classic 2048 puzzle, where artificial intelligence meets traditional gameplay. Instead of using a keyboard or mouse, you control the game through **hand gestures** in front of your webcam.

## ‚ú® Features
* **AI Hand Tracking:** Uses computer vision to detect hand movements in real-time.
* **Gesture Control:** Swipe up, down, left, or right in the air to move the tiles.
* **Smooth Experience:** Designed for a "flow" state with responsive animations.
* **No Touch Required:** A completely touchless way to play a classic puzzle.

## üìñ How to Play
1.  Open the [Live Demo](https://2048-flow-game.netlify.app/).
2.  Allow camera access when prompted.
3.  Hold your hand in front of the camera.
4.  Move your hand quickly (swipe) in the direction you want the tiles to shift.
5.  Combine tiles with the same number to reach **2048**!
